Case Study

Fuel

Brand identity and packaging design for a non-alcoholic energy craft beer

Logo Design Brand Identity Packaging Design Web Design

Project Overview

Fuel is an innovative non-alcoholic energy craft beer that aims to provide the authentic taste of regular beer while delivering an energy boost rather than intoxication. This unique product targets beer enthusiasts who want to enjoy the flavor experience without the alcohol effects, as well as active individuals looking for an energizing beverage with a familiar taste profile.

The project involved creating a comprehensive brand identity, packaging design, and marketing materials that would position Fuel as a premium, energizing alternative in the craft beer market. The design needed to communicate both the beer heritage and the energy-boosting properties of the product.

Project Details

  • Timeline 6 weeks
  • Industry Beverage / Craft Beer
  • Client Fuel Beverages
  • My Role Graphic Designer, Project Manager

The Challenge

The main challenge was to create a brand identity that effectively communicated the dual nature of the product: a non-alcoholic beer with energy-boosting properties. The design needed to appeal to both craft beer enthusiasts and energy drink consumers, two markets with distinct visual languages and expectations.

  • Balance craft beer aesthetics with energy drink dynamism
  • Differentiate from both alcoholic beers and traditional energy drinks
  • Create packaging that stands out in both beverage categories

The Solution

I developed a bold, energetic brand identity that incorporates elements from both craft beer culture and energy products. The logo combines an oil drop (representing fuel) with fire (symbolizing energy) to create a distinctive mark that communicates the product's unique value proposition.

  • Created a dynamic logo that visually represents the product's dual benefits
  • Designed packaging that borrows from craft beer aesthetics but with energetic elements
  • Developed a cohesive system across packaging, web, and point-of-purchase materials

Research & Process

I began by analyzing existing craft beer brands and energy drinks to understand their marketing strategies and design styles. This research helped identify opportunities to create a unique visual identity that would stand out in both markets while appealing to the target audience.

The distinctive packaging and marketing materials helped establish a strong presence in retail environments.
