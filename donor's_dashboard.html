Case Study

Love in Action Donor's Dashboard

A comprehensive dashboard for donors to track their contributions and the impact they make

Dashboard Design UI/UX Design Web Development

Project Overview

Love in Action is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of children across the globe. This project involved designing and developing a comprehensive dashboard for donors to track their contributions, view detailed information about sponsored children, and access overall statistics about the organization's activities and impact.

The dashboard is structured to provide a seamless experience for donors, with sections for overall statistics, detailed profiles of sponsored children, donation history, and account management. The goal was to create an intuitive and user-friendly interface that would enhance donor engagement and transparency.

Project Details

  • Timeline 8 weeks
  • Industry Non-profit
  • Client Love in Action
  • My Role UI/UX Designer, Front-end Developer

The Challenge

The main challenge was to create a dashboard that would effectively communicate complex information about donations, sponsored children, and organizational impact in a clear, engaging, and user-friendly manner. The system needed to cater to donors of varying technical abilities while providing comprehensive information.

  • Present complex financial and impact data in an accessible way
  • Create an emotional connection between donors and sponsored children
  • Design a system that works across various devices and screen sizes

The Solution

I designed a comprehensive dashboard with a clean, intuitive interface that prioritizes key information while providing easy access to more detailed data. The solution includes data visualizations, personalized profiles of sponsored children, and a clear donation history to enhance transparency and engagement.

  • Created intuitive data visualizations to communicate impact
  • Developed personalized profiles for each sponsored child
  • Implemented a responsive design that works seamlessly across devices

Design Process

1

Research & Planning

Conducted user research to understand donor needs and expectations. Analyzed existing dashboard solutions and created a comprehensive site map to outline the information architecture.

2

Wireframing & Prototyping

Created detailed wireframes to establish the layout and structure of the dashboard. Developed interactive prototypes to test user flows and gather feedback.

3

UI Design & Development

Designed the final user interface with a focus on clarity, accessibility, and emotional connection. Developed the front-end using modern web technologies to ensure a responsive and performant experience.

Wireframing & Information Architecture

Wireframe Sketches

Initial wireframes were created to outline the structure and layout of the dashboard, ensuring a user-centric design approach. These sketches helped establish the hierarchy of information and key user flows.

Site Map

A comprehensive site map was developed to illustrate the hierarchy and navigation flow of the dashboard, providing a clear guide for the user experience and ensuring all necessary information was accessible.

Final Dashboard Design

The final dashboard design features a clean, intuitive interface with four main sections: Analytics Dashboard, Your Children, Donation History, and User Profile. Each section is designed to provide clear information while maintaining a cohesive visual language.

Analytics Dashboard

Overview of key metrics and impact statistics

Your Children

Profiles and updates on sponsored children

Donation History

Detailed record of past contributions

User Profile

Account management and personal information

Final Mockup

The dashboard was implemented as a responsive web application, ensuring a seamless experience across desktop and mobile devices. The final design maintains a balance between visual appeal and functional clarity.

Results & Impact

The donor dashboard has significantly enhanced the transparency and engagement of Love in Action's donor community. By providing clear visibility into the impact of donations and creating a personal connection with sponsored children, the dashboard has helped improve donor retention and satisfaction.

Donor Engagement

Increased donor engagement and interaction with the platform

Retention Rate

Improved donor retention through enhanced transparency

Emotional Connection

Stronger bonds between donors and sponsored children

User Satisfaction

Positive feedback on the intuitive and informative interface
