Case Study

Love in Action

Brand identity for a non-profit organization empowering underprivileged children in Ethiopia

Brand Identity Marketing Materials Stationery Design

Project Overview

This project involved creating a comprehensive brand identity for Love in Action, a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering underprivileged children in Boreda, Ethiopia. The goal was to develop a visual identity that represents the organization's commitment to transforming lives through education, healthcare, and essential resources.

Love in Action works to create sustainable change in communities through transformative projects and initiatives, focusing on long-term impact rather than temporary solutions.

Project Details

  • Timeline 4 weeks
  • Industry Non-profit
  • Client Love in Action
  • Location Boreda, Ethiopia

The Challenge

The main challenge was to design a brand identity that captures the organization's mission and core values while ensuring that their message is effectively communicated to supporters and stakeholders. The branding needed to evoke hope, compassion, and a sense of community while maintaining professionalism and credibility.

  • Create a visual identity that resonates with both Western donors and local communities
  • Develop a brand that communicates hope and transformation
  • Design materials that work across digital and print platforms

The Solution

I developed a comprehensive brand identity that embodies the organization's mission of transforming lives through love and action. The visual system includes a logo, color palette, typography, and various applications that effectively communicate the organization's values and purpose.

  • Created a logo that symbolizes growth, care, and community
  • Developed a warm, inviting color palette that reflects Ethiopian culture
  • Designed versatile marketing materials for fundraising and awareness

Design Process

1

Research & Discovery

Conducted in-depth research on the organization's mission, values, and target audience. Explored Ethiopian cultural elements and non-profit design trends to inform the creative direction.

2

Brand Identity Development

Created a logo, color palette, and typography system that embodies the organization's mission. Developed visual elements that communicate hope, transformation, and community.

3

Application & Implementation

Applied the brand identity to various marketing materials, including stationery, brochures, social media templates, and fundraising materials to ensure consistency across all touchpoints.

Results & Impact

The new brand identity has significantly enhanced Love in Action's ability to communicate their mission and connect with supporters. The cohesive visual system has helped establish a strong presence in both digital and print media.

Donor Engagement

Increased donor engagement and support for fundraising campaigns

Brand Recognition

Improved brand recognition among target audiences

Community Trust

Strengthened trust and credibility within the community

Clear Messaging

More effective communication of the organization's mission
